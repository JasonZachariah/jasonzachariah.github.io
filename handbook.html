CONTEXT & RESEARCH

To understand the issues design students faces, I surveyed 15 current DESN students to understand their expeirnce wwhen planning out their degree.

Here is what I gained:

Confusion over course requirements

The current Design handbook lacks support to showing how courses affect their degree, with 53% of having trouble keeping track of course requirments, and 46% wishing they had a real-time degree requirements checker

Planning your degree is tedious.

The current system of planning out the degree requires students to go through 3 separate processes to plan their program,creating a split task flow that has a higher rate of miscommunication. Over 25% of students say they have to rely on other schedule trackers (Notion,Excel etc)to keep track of the 3 diffrent task flows .

Problem

Inconsistant, divided platforms

Solution

One dashboard, for all your course planning needs

Method

A/B testing

Since I wanted to test I wide scope of features to improve the work, I used A/B resting amgonst 3 students in order to make clearer desgin choices that optimize orginzation and faster workflow speeds.
