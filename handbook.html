The Problem

Planning out your courses is too spread out.

The DESN Program is a 120-credit program that requires students to plan out their courses to fit the requirements. The current system of planning out the degree requires students to go through 3 separate processes to plan their program- one for checking degree requirements, one for checking availability of courses, and one for plotting their timetables. This split-up system creates potential for miscommunication and a chance for students to fail to maintain degree requirements.
