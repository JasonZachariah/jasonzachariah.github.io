My Philosophy

Working as a product design and after-school coding teacher for 3 years now, I have watched how technologies have evolved over the years, and the impacts these technologies have had on our next generation. With the rise of emergent technology, such as AI technologies that have shaped us, we have failed to realize how these technologies can affect us in the long term. My approach is not to dissuade these technologies from existing, but rather to design for a more well-thought-out approach for their implementation in everyday life.

My Experience

I have worked at WizRobotics as a UX/UI designer, covering areas such as branding for B2B services, prototyping platforms for AI technology, and collaborating with developers to create a systematic approach to designing their platforms. Working at a small company has taught me how to wear many hats at once, and more importantly, how to advocate for my ideas in a small environment.
