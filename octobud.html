The Problem

Technology in the classroom divides more than it helps

With more than 43% of Gen Alpha owning some form of technology before kindergarten, kids are becoming more accustomed to technology in their daily life, including their education. However, with the prevalence of methods such as Chromebooks or iPads, which lack proper ways of fostering a cooperative learning environment, there now exists a barrier between students and teachers when it comes to learning digitally. So, how do we allow students to be motivated using technology while also allowing teachers to maintain a flexible and easy-to-maintain way of teaching?
